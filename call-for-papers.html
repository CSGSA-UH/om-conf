RACSR 2023

Call for Papers

We invite researchers and practitioners from this class to participate in the 1st Conference on Recent Advances on Computer Science Resarch (RACSR, â€˜23). This conference aims to provide a platform for exchanging ideas, discussing recent developments, and presenting research findings in all areas of computer science. The conference welcomes submissions of original research papers, and among the topics of interest are, but are not limited to:

  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Cybersecurity
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Computational Biology
  • Robotics
  • Automata
  • Cloud and Networking
  • Wireless Networks
  • Software Engineering
  • Embedded Systems and Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Quantum Computing
  • Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Real-Time Systems
  • Computer Graphics

A program committee made up of subject-matter experts will evaluate the submissions. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, among these papers, some will be selected for a contributed talk and others for poster presentation.

Important Dates:

  • Paper Submission Deadline: April 9, 2023
  • Notification of Reviews: April 18, 2023
  • Conference Dates: April 26, 2023

We look forward to your participation in the RACSR 23. Please visit our website for more information on submission guidelines, and venue details.
